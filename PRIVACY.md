# Cartograph Privacy Policy

Cartograph is an offline-only macOS app.

Cartograph does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not include advertising, and does not contact external servers.

Files you create, save, open, or export stay on your Mac unless you choose to share them yourself through macOS or another app.

Cartograph uses the macOS App Sandbox and only requests user-selected file access for opening, saving, and exporting maps.
